Covid-19 Awareness Toolkit




Since March 2020, Reliance Foundation has been working swiftly and at scale with multi-pronged efforts to strengthen the nation’s fight against COVID-19. Through these initiatives, Reliance Foundation has also built up a resource of validated information on COVID-19 awareness and prevention, working with in-house medical experts as well as with globally and nationally ratified information. A toolkit of these awareness materials relating to Safety and Prevention, Health and General Wellbeing has been collated here. Free to download, use as well as circulate, these materials are intended to support any knowledge creation and awareness efforts related to the coronavirus pandemic.
